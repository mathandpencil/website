Founders

Math & Pencil was started by Joseph Misiti and Timothy Hungerford in 2011. The company exists to be the architects, engineers and designers behind a plethora of digital business developments - If you can dream up the idea - we can build it with you.

  • the Pencil

    Tim can be found climbing rocks, trekking international landscapes, surfing off shore breaks, chopping wood with his father-in-law or riding his bike through rain, snow and shine... all which usually include his amazing wife, beautiful daughter, family and friends. He is the Chief Designer at Fetcher and Dukemail as well as an owner of Math & Pencil.

    Tim studied marketing at Indiana University and has designed and built applications for medical devices, bicycle shops, farms and myriads of other online products and businesses. He resides in Western New York, splitting time between Buffalo and the small town of Medina - where he's active in the restoration of 19th century historic buildings. You'll often find him alongside Joe enjoying wine and cheap beer while listening to the sounds of Hop Along, Joe Pug, Pat Carroll, Saintseneca, Tom Waits and so on.
