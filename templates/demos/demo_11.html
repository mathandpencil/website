Donors

Where Your Support Fosters Passion and Progress

Make a Gift

For over a quarter century, the New York Academy of Art has been a vital proponent of the creation, display, and teaching of figurative art. A not-for-profit institution serving more than one hundred artists in residence and thousands of visitors annually, the Academy relies on the generosity of individuals, companies, foundations and charitable organizations.

Become a Patron

Making a contribution of $1,000 or more, members of the New York Academy of Art’s Patron Program are key allies in our effort to create a home for the most distinguished artists the world has to offer. Your support will help us enrich our course offerings, attract prominent teaching artists from around the globe, and allow us to provide crucial merit-based scholarships to students who exhibit financial need.

Corporate Partnerships

The artistic community is a diverse and vibrant group and we are proud to bring them all together under one roof—be it a first time art buyer combing through an exhibition, a long time enthusiast indulging in one of our master classes, or a student just arrived for their first day at the Academy.
